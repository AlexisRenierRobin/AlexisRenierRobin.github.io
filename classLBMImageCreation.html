Creates a image with a gaussian.

The formula used to create the gaussian image is :

\begin{align*} I(i,\,j) = 255 - A * exp \left(- \frac{ \left(i - centerN \right)^{2} \, \left(j - centerP\right)^{2}}{2\sigma^{2}} \right) \end{align*}

with :

  • i : the height index of the pixel
  • j : the width index of the pixel
  • centerN : height position of the center of the gaussian
  • centerP : width position of the center of the gaussian
  • A : amplitude of the gaussian
  • \(\sigma\) : standard deviation of the gaussian
