Computes the GSSIM between two images.

The SSIM formula is :

\begin{align*} SSIM(x,\,y) = \left[l(x,\,y)\right]^{\alpha} \cdot \left[c(x,\,y)\right]^{\beta} \cdot \left[s(x,\,y)\right]^{\gamma} = \left[\frac{2\mu_{x}\mu_{y} + c_{1}}{\mu_{x}^{2} + \mu_{y}^{2} + c_{1}}\right]^{\alpha} \cdot \left[\frac{2\sigma_{x}\sigma_{y} + c_{2}}{\sigma_{x}^{2} + \sigma_{y}^{2} + c_{2}}\right]^{\beta} \cdot \left[\frac{cov_{xy} + c_{3}}{\sigma_{x}\sigma_{y} + c_{3}}\right]^{\gamma} \end{align*}

where :

\begin{align*} &\bullet \; \; \mu_{x}\;is\;the\;mean\;of\;x \\ &\bullet \; \; \mu_{y}\;is\;the\;mean\;of\;y \\ &\bullet \; \; \sigma_{x}^{2}\;is\;the\;variance\;of\;x \\ &\bullet \; \; \sigma_{y}^{2}\;is\;the\;variance\;of\;y \\ &\bullet \; \; cov_{xy}\;is\;the\;covariance\;of\;x\;and\;y \\ &\bullet \; \; c_{1} = \left(k_{1}L \right)^{2},\;c_{2} = \left(k_{2}L \right)^{2}\;and\;c_{3} = \frac{c_{2}}{2} \\ &\bullet \; \; L\;the\;dynamic\;range\;of\;the\;pixel\;values\;(here\;255) \\ &\bullet \; \; k_{1} = 0.01,\;k_{2}=0.03,\;\alpha=\beta=\gamma=0 \\ \end{align*}

The GSSIM is computed by applying the SSIM formula on all the image at once

Parameters
pImageOriginalA pointer to the original image
pImageModifiedA pointer to the modified image
applyFilterOnOriginalImageFlag, specifying whether to apply a gaussian filter on orginal image or not
applyFilterOnModifiedImageFlag, specifying whether to apply a gaussian filter on modified image or not
Returns
The computed metric

Computes the MSSIM between two images.

The SSIM formula is :

\begin{align*} SSIM(x,\,y) = \left[l(x,\,y)\right]^{\alpha} \cdot \left[c(x,\,y)\right]^{\beta} \cdot \left[s(x,\,y)\right]^{\gamma} = \left[\frac{2\mu_{x}\mu_{y} + c_{1}}{\mu_{x}^{2} + \mu_{y}^{2} + c_{1}}\right]^{\alpha} \cdot \left[\frac{2\sigma_{x}\sigma_{y} + c_{2}}{\sigma_{x}^{2} + \sigma_{y}^{2} + c_{2}}\right]^{\beta} \cdot \left[\frac{cov_{xy} + c_{3}}{\sigma_{x}\sigma_{y} + c_{3}}\right]^{\gamma} \end{align*}

where :

\begin{align*} &\bullet \; \; \mu_{x}\;is\;the\;mean\;of\;x \\ &\bullet \; \; \mu_{y}\;is\;the\;mean\;of\;y \\ &\bullet \; \; \sigma_{x}^{2}\;is\;the\;variance\;of\;x \\ &\bullet \; \; \sigma_{y}^{2}\;is\;the\;variance\;of\;y \\ &\bullet \; \; cov_{xy}\;is\;the\;covariance\;of\;x\;and\;y \\ &\bullet \; \; c_{1} = \left(k_{1}L \right)^{2},\;c_{2} = \left(k_{2}L \right)^{2}\;and\;c_{3} = \frac{c_{2}}{2} \\ &\bullet \; \; L\;the\;dynamic\;range\;of\;the\;pixel\;values\;(here\;255) \\ &\bullet \; \; k_{1} = 0.01,\;k_{2}=0.03,\;\alpha=\beta=\gamma=0 \\ \end{align*}

The MSSIM is computed by applying the SSIM on 11x11 squares all over the image. The MSSIM is the mean of the SSIM computed on all squares.

pImageModifiedA pointer to the modified image

Computes the MWLE between two images.

The MLWE is calculated with the foloowing formula :

\[MLWE=\left[ \frac{1}{25} \sum_{i \, = \, 1}^{5} \sum_{i \, = \, 1}^{5} \left| \frac{e_{ij}}{W_{ij}} \right|^{2} \right]^{1/2} \]

where :

\begin{align*} &\bullet \; \; f_{ij} : pixel\;of\;original\;image \\ &\bullet \; \; h_{ij} : pixel\;of\;halftoned\;image \\ &\bullet \; \; e_{ij} = f_{ij} - h{ij} \\ &\bullet \; \; W_{ij} = exp(k \, M_{ij})\;with\;k \, = \, 0.003 \\ &\bullet \; \; M_{ij} = \sum_{i = 1}^{3} \sum_{j = 1}^{3} \left| h_{ij} - m \right|\;where\;m\;is\;the\;mean\;in\;a\;3x3\;neighborhood\;around\;the\;pixel\;at\;location\;(i,\;j) \end{align*}

The function computes MLWE all over the image and return the biggest
